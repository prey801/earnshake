FAQ

Questions Students Ask

Do I have to do any of the actual work? +
Not at all. Our team of professional writers and researchers handles 100% of the tasks completed through your account. Your only job is to sign up and collect your weekly payment.
Is this against Handshake AI's terms? +
We operate carefully and securely. Every account is accessed through a residential proxy that matches your location, so activity appears normal. We manage volume to avoid triggering any flags on your account.
Why do you take 60% and I only get 40%? +
Our 60% covers residential proxy costs, the wages of our professional writers who do all the actual work, platform operations, and client support. You provide the account — we provide everything else. You earn $0 effort money, we earn $0 without your account.
How much can I realistically earn per month? +
Most students earn between $80 and $350/month depending on their account's subject area and task availability. STEM and technical accounts tend to earn more. Use the calculator above to estimate your specific earnings.
How do I get paid? +
Every Friday we send your earnings via Venmo, Zelle, or CashApp — whichever you prefer. You'll also receive a weekly earnings summary showing exactly what was completed and what you earned.
Can I stop whenever I want? +
Yes, absolutely. No contracts, no lock-in period, no cancellation fees. Just message us and we'll stop accessing your account within the hour. Any unpaid earnings will still be sent on the next Friday payout.
I'm an international student on F-1 visa — can I still join? +
Yes. Since you're not performing any work yourself, this is passive income from an account — not employment. However, we always recommend checking with your university's DSO office if you have specific visa concerns.
